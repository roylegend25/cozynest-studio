About Us

Crafting Spaces
That Feel Like Home

At CozyNest Studio, we believe interiors should embrace you with warmth while celebrating clean, intentional design. Our Japandi-meets-Mediterranean philosophy blends raw natural materials, soft arches, and sun-kissed palettes to create sanctuaries of calm.

Every project is a dialogue — between light and shadow, texture and smoothness, tradition and modernity. We don't just decorate; we compose atmospheres.

What We Do

Our Services

01

False Ceiling

Sculptural ceiling designs that transform overhead space into an architectural statement. We use layered gypsum, warm cove lighting, and soft geometric forms to add depth and intimacy to any room.

02

Bedroom Design

Restful retreats wrapped in soft linens, warm wood tones, and gentle lighting. Our bedroom designs prioritize tranquility — layered textures, muted palettes, and bespoke storage that disappears into the architecture.

03

Living Room Design

The heart of the home — designed for conversation, comfort, and quiet admiration. We curate furniture layouts that flow naturally, with focal points that draw the eye and materials that invite touch.
